About Me

Welcome to my personal page!

Hello and welcome! My name is Ohom Bhattacharjee, and I’m currently a student in Class 6. While I may be young, my
passion for coding is something that runs deep and grows stronger every day. I believe that age is no barrier to
creativity or innovation, and this portfolio is a reflection of my journey so far in the world of technology.
My adventure into coding began with block-based platforms like Scratch and Code.org, where I learned the basics of
programming logic and structure through fun and interactive projects. These platforms gave me the confidence to explore
more advanced tools and languages. Now, I actively work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create real websites, design
user interfaces, and experiment with interactive elements on the web.
One of the things I love most about coding is the freedom it gives me to create anything I imagine—from colorful,
animated pages to small web-based games and tools. Every project teaches me something new, whether it’s how to structure
a website, how to make it look nice with CSS, or how to make it respond to user actions with JavaScript.
As a student, I’m constantly learning—not just in school, but also through online tutorials, coding challenges, and by
studying other developers’ work. I spend a lot of my free time exploring new ideas, solving coding puzzles, and building
small projects that help me practice what I’ve learned. I enjoy the process of debugging, refining, and improving my
work, and I never stop looking for ways to make my code cleaner and more effective.
My goal is to keep expanding my skills and eventually learn more advanced programming languages, like Python or C++, and
maybe even get into app or game development one day. I’m especially interested in creating things that are both useful
and fun, and I hope that someday my work will help or inspire others.
This portfolio is not just a collection of projects—it’s a story of my growth as a young developer. Here, you'll find
some of the things I’ve created, the technologies I’ve used, and the progress I’ve made as I continue to learn and
build. I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I’ve enjoyed creating it.
Thank you for visiting my website. I’m excited about the future, and this is just the beginning!
