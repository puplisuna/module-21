Our Location

Find us at the heart of the city, nestled on a scenic hilltop.

Mount Carmel Hill Restaurant
123 Hilltop Avenue,
Near Green Park,
Cityville, State 123456
India
How to Reach Us:
  • From City Center Bus Stand: Take the Green Line bus towards Hilltop. Get down at "Green Park" stop. The restaurant is a 5-minute walk uphill from the stop.
  • From Railway Station: Hire a taxi or auto-rickshaw to "Mount Carmel Hill Restaurant, Hilltop Avenue". The drive takes about 20 minutes.
  • By Car: Follow the main road towards Green Park. After the park, take the first right onto Hilltop Avenue. Free parking is available at the restaurant.
  • Landmark: We are right next to the famous Green Park and opposite Cityville Public Library.
